Common Questions

Why not just use something you already have?

Why not just use C2PA / Content Credentials?

C2PA embeds a manifest that proves an AI created a piece of media. AIAuth proves that a human reviewed the AI's output before it shipped. These answer different questions — if you care about provenance AND accountability, you want both. AIAuth reads C2PA manifests when present and records them in the receipt.

Why not just use an AI governance platform?

Governance platforms manage the AI system: model registry, risk scoring, policy publication. AIAuth manages the last mile: what happened after the AI produced output and before a human shipped it. If you already run a governance platform, AIAuth is the attestation engine that plugs into it. If you don't, AIAuth is the cheapest way to satisfy the compliance questions most governance platforms exist to answer.

Why not just trust the AI?

Because your regulator, your client, and your malpractice insurer won't. EU AI Act Article 50, emerging state-level requirements in the US, and professional-liability frameworks in finance, law, and healthcare all expect a verifiable record of human review. AIAuth is the shortest path from "we use AI" to "we can prove a human reviewed every use."
