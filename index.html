What it is

A signed receipt every time you ship AI work.

Built for knowledge workers whose output increasingly runs through an AI, and for the clients, auditors, and employers who need to trust it. Your content is hashed on your device; only the hash is sent to AIAuth to be signed.

01

Private by design

Content never leaves your machine. We only see a SHA-256 fingerprint and forget it after signing.

02

Works across every AI

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, or any tool. One Chrome extension, one keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+A.

03

Any format, not just text

Drop a spreadsheet, PDF, image, or any file onto AIAuth. Same cryptographic receipt, same privacy guarantee.

How it works

One signature. One receipt. Full accountability.

Step 01

Create

Use any AI tool. When you're satisfied with the output, select the text or pick a file.

Step 02

Sign

Press Ctrl+Shift+A. AIAuth hashes your content locally and signs the hash with an Ed25519 key.

Step 03

Ship

Paste the short receipt code [AIAuth:…] next to your work. Share the full JSON receipt with anyone who needs to verify.

Step 04

Verify

Anyone can paste the code or receipt at aiauth.app/check. They'll see who attested it, when, and whether the signature is intact.
